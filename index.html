Word shuffler

Word shuffler enables you to shuffle the letters of a word in to a random order. Simply enter the word you'd like to shuffle in the textarea below and click "Shuffle". The result will come up in the grey area under the button.

With Word shuffler you can also shuffle entire sentences or paragraphs. The words will be shuffled one at a time so that sentence structure is intact. The first letter of a sentence will always be capitalized, even after it's been shuffled. A word that was written in all capital letters before it got shuffled will also stay that way.

Word shuffler only works with the English alphabet, numbers and question marks, exclamation marks and dots [?!.]. All other characters will be deleted before the shuffling takes place.
Only works with English letters, numbers and characters "!", "?" and ".".
